I dressed up an alt avatar as a dog using one of the Jakkal collies and 
have been driving it with libSL.  Commands are accepted via chat.  I 
used the AppThatMustNotBeNamed as the basis for this.  I thought it 
would be nice to tell my dog, "Go buy me a beer," and that sort of 
thing.  Don't get too excited, since it's just prototypical code, but 
it's fun.

Actually, since the animations and attachments for avatars vastly 
surpass in quantity and quality all of the inanimate/prim-based pets, 
it's very nice.  And since the avatar code runs just fine on a much 
smaller, slower machine, it's a big win all 'round.
