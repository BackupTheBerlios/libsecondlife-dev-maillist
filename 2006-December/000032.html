I know that announced dates for things don't hold much weight in SL (or 
the net in general), but rumors circulating around say a beta client 
will be released next Wednesday that implements the new REST (https) 
protocol for all of the reliable client->viewer transactions. This would 
be a very good thing and make libsecondlife much more reliable and easy 
to write, but it also means a significant rewrite of most of the 
library. The beta period will probably last a while so when it's 
released we'll fork the code, and hopefully we can gather as many 
programmers, reverse engineers, documentation writers, and testers as 
possible for the rewrite.

Get your rest, I'll see you next week! :-)
