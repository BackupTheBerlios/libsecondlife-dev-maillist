Summary: Prim XML (De)serialization is Broken

Details: We have not yet been successful is deserializing a list of prims from XML. The serialization appears to work correctly and multiple prims are output in to a list along with all of their parameters, but when deserializing back in to a List<PrimObject> structure lots of error callbacks occur, failing on nearly everything that is not an attribute.

Maybe we have to generate an XSD (using xsd.exe included with the .NET 2.0 SDK) and use that for deserialization? More investigation is needed here.
